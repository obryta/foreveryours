How it began

Our Story

♡

What started as a chance encounter at a mutual friend's gathering in Accra became the beginning of something neither Kwame nor Abena could have imagined. A quiet conversation about music, food, and life's big questions stretched through the night until the stars themselves seemed to pause and listen.

Kwame, a civil engineer with a love for long drives and jollof debates, found in Abena not just a partner, but his truest companion. Abena, a graphic designer whose creativity fills every room she enters, found in Kwame a steady warmth and a laugh that always feels like home.

Three years, countless memories, and one unforgettable sunset proposal at Labadi Beach later — they said yes to forever. Now they invite you, their most cherished family and friends, to witness the beginning of this beautiful chapter.
